How to play

Rules

The playing board is composed of tiles that may or may not hide a bomb underneath. The objective of the game is to find and reveal all the tiles that do not contain a bomb.

Each tile once uncovered reveals the information on how many tiles adjacent to it contain a bomb. This information is conveyed through a number written on the tile. "Adjacent tile" means any one of the 8 tiles surrounding a given tile. If an uncovered tile doesn't have a number then none of the adjacent tiles hide a bomb.

In order to aid yourself with remembering which tiles certainly contain a bomb you can flag a tile. A flagged tile is displayed with "F" written on top of it. Flagging a tile also decreases the "Bombs left" counter. Uncovering an already uncovered tile if around it there are at least as many flagged tiles as the number on this tile suggests, reveals all the adjacent tiles that are not flagged.

First unveiling guarantees a tile with no bombs in adjacent tiles.

Controls

Left click or short press - reveal a tile or tiles adjacent to it

Right click or long press - flag or unflag a tile
